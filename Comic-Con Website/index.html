Interactive Sets

Become an Avenger, or be a part of the Walking Dead- using our cosplay photobackdrops! We have numerous interactive sets and photo ops where you can get some amazing photos (for no extra charge!).

Guest Signings

One of the most popular areas is our guest signing area which is located on the second level of our venue. Here you can get your memorabilia signed, get a signed photo or just chat to your favorite guest!

Cosplay Village

The Cosplay Village is a must-visit attraction, where you can find amazing costumes, a costume repair station, sign up for costume contests, or simply come and use our free photo backdrops.

Costume Contest

DCC always has some amazing costumers and cosplayers in attendance and we love to give them a chance to show how much work they have put into their costumes and win some prizes.

Escape Rooms, VR, 360 Cameras and more!

From 360 cameras to capture your pose from all sides to Escape Rooms, Ravens, Owls and much more!

Panels/Q&A'S

Each year all our special guests hold a panel where they talk about their work and you can ask them any burning questions you have.

Craft Tables

We love artists..those of all types! We also dedicate a large space to artists of different mediums from leatherworkers and sculptors to seamstress's and jewelry makers.

Tabletop Gaming

On our second floor you can test your wits in various table top and card games with our official Table Top Gaming Partner, Sandbox Gaming.

Artist Alley

We love artists and as such we dedicate a large space for indepdent artists (no big retailers here!) to showcase and sell their work aswell as give you a chance to commission some unique artwork.

Traders Hall

Every year DCC has retailers from all over Ireland and Europe attend and they bring with them a host of goods from high quality statues to vintage figures and comic books to plushies! There is something for everyone!
