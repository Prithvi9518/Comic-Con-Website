Brand Opportunities

You can see some of the previous brands DCC has worked with over the years on either side of this page.

Do you want your brand associated with Ireland's largest and fastest growing fandom event?

We have worked with companies such as RTE 2FM, Dublin City Council, Entertainment.ie, Universal Pictures, JustEat.ie, TeeBusters, Cineworld, Ubisoft, Nintendo and more.

With 20,000 + attendees at each of our Dublin Comic Con event's (not counting our other Comic Con Ireland run events) we provide a unique and wide-reaching platform for you to target a wide demographic. From die-hard ( and tech savvy ) fans and families to casual fans, we attract a large new and returning customer base as well as an ever-growing fan base.

The impact, exposure and reach that Comic Con Ireland events have provided for advertisers, partners and sponsors is unparalleled. Comic Con Ireland events enjoy a large and dedicated media base, with long-standing relationships at national and international press outlets that guarantees coverage for every event.

From the headline, wristband and guest sponsorship to digital ads and hanging banners, we have many advertising options to suit your budget.

Should you wish to learn more or have queries relating to press, PR, advertising or sponsorship be sure to email Olivia.Fahy@dublincomiccon.com (PR/Press) or karl.walsh@dublincomiccon.com (Advertising/Sponsorship)
